Core Brand Values

100% genuine Sri Lankan origin. We pride ourselves on delivering the true taste of our homeland's rich agricultural heritage without artificial alterations.

purity

No added sugars, no artificial preservatives. Our cold-pressed methods and natural drying techniques preserve the vital nutrients and vitamins inherently present in the fruit.

Excellence

Adhering strictly to export-quality standards. From the plantation to the packaging, every step is rigorously monitored to ensure a premium product.
